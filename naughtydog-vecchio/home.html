PRESS RELEASE! Gex 2 Lead Designer Joins Naughty Dog

So you want to be a Naughty Dog , do you?

You want to run with the pack ... doing what you want, when you want... making some of the hottest video games out there... and then having them published by the best ...

...not likely...

You have to be in the doghouse 24 hours a day hopped up on caffeine, listening to Justin's "who the f*#@ is that" music, two days from a shower, arguing with your spouse/significant other/whatever on the phone about the definition of "reasonable hours," while cranking out code or art EVERY SINGLE DAY, consistently, for two years straight, to even begin to understand the lifestyle.

You might not want to...

...then again, you might.

We love it.

After all...only the lead dog gets to change the view.

And that is why we put together this web page. So you can get familiar with Naughty Dog from the comfort of your own home/office, on your own schedule, and without Justin's music.
Clicking the Paw to the right will take you to the Naughty Dog " corporate biography ". That's fancy talk for our history. Get the low down on how this all came to pass, and where we want it to go.

That little orange guy to the left is Crash Bandicoot . If you didn't already know that, then welcome back from life support! Click on his mug to find out more than you care to know about him.

Garage Days ... Old games made on the quick... every good developer has a passel of them. Our parents were kind enough to let us use the basement instead of the garage. It was colder, sure, but a lot less greasy. We traded Carbon Monoxide for Radon, but nobody ever said making games was easy. Click Naughty Dog to the left to go to The Kennel . Don't tease the mutts -- they bite. Meet the motley crew we call The Pack. Photographs of team members are included... we apologize in advance. Note for headhunters: Don't even bother.

Want to leave us a message ? Drop one on the hydrant to the right! No game ideas from anyone, please. Especially from friends of our parents.
